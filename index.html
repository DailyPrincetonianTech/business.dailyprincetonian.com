    What is the ‘Prince’?
    The Daily Princetonian is Princeton University’s leading daily publication. Founded in 1876, the ‘Prince’ is student-run and operates independently of the University. Though our focus has traditionally been on the print newspaper, we’ve wholeheartedly embraced online journalism, with over 1.5 million site visits in the first half of 2020. We’ve gained a larger audience through social media platforms like Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter. When classes are in session, our email newsletter reaches nearly three thousand subscribers every weekday (weekly during the summer). The ‘Prince’ strives to achieve high journalistic standards and drive on-campus dialogue about pressing issues.

    Due to COVID-19, the ‘Prince’ is not printing this summer. During the upcoming school year, ‘The Prince’ will mail a monthly copy to all students and other Princeton community members. Contact the business​@dailyprincetonian.com for details.

    48U Studios
     
    For over a century, the ‘Prince' has helped businesses recruit talent from the Princeton undergraduate and graduate student bodies through traditional advertising. Today, we are excited to offer premium custom content through 48U Studios. 48U Studios offers recruiters creative ways to stand out from the hundreds of businesses that attend Princeton career fairs and subscribe to online recruiting services. From articles about daily work life to feature video interviews with current employees, the creative staff at 48U Studios will work with you to reach Princeton students. To see the 48U Studios portfolio, or to inquire about working with us, email us at business@dailyprincetonian.com.

    Fall Special Issues
     
    This fall, the ‘Prince’ will be producing three special print issues. We are grateful to have the opportunity to tie our remote Princeton community together; each monthly issue will be mailed to the homes of every enrolled undergraduate and graduate Princeton student (approx. 8,000). This edition will be in color, and will be printed on around forty 11x11” pages. Since we will be sending them to the printer the last Sunday of each month, we ask that all advertising requests and artwork are submitted two Fridays before those dates: 09/11, 10/11, and 11/27. Thank you for advertising with the ‘Prince’, and please reach out with any questions!
